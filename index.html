Upward Bound Alumnis

Madera Community College's Upward Bound program is a college-prep program that serves students by helping them prepare for, attend, and finish college. Upward Bound provides low-income and First Generation students opportunities to succeed in precollege preformances and in getting a higher education. First Generation Alumnis are individuals who are the first in their families that have gone through a Upward Bound program and completed college. There are many that do not stop at one college but go to multiple to achieve a higher degree in education.
